Tirzepatide Plus

Once-Weekly Weight Loss Injection

Plentiful Health & Wellness provides compounded tirzepatide for weight loss. Tirzepatide Plus is a once-weekly injection for the treatment of obesity. It is a single peptide that triggers the body's receptors for GIP and GLP-1 — two natural gut hormones. Cyanocobalamin (B12) is included to help increase energy.

GIP has decreased food intake and increased energy expenditure, resulting in weight reductions. Combined with a GLP-1 receptor agonist, it has a more significant effect on glucose and body weight. Tirzepatide Plus is sold per vial, each of which typically lasts one month and is shipped directly to you.
