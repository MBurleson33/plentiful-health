Weight Loss Injection

Tirzepatide Plus

Once-Weekly · Shipped Directly to You

Plentiful Health & Wellness provides compounded tirzepatide for weight loss. Tirzepatide Plus is a once-weekly injection for the treatment of obesity — a single peptide that triggers the body's receptors for GIP and GLP-1, two natural gut hormones. Cyanocobalamin (B12) is included to help increase energy.

GIP has decreased food intake and increased energy expenditure, resulting in weight reductions. Combined with a GLP-1 receptor agonist, it has a more significant effect on glucose and body weight. Sold per vial — each typically lasts one month and is shipped directly to you.
